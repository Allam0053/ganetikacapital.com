About Us

Our philosophy, our principles, and our partnership.

Ganetika Capital Partners

Ganetika Capital Partners is a private investment partnership focused on the long-term ownership of high-quality businesses. The partnership invests in both Indonesian and global markets, seeking opportunities where price and value diverge. Our approach is rooted in sound business judgment, disciplined capital allocation, and a genuine partnership ethos. Ganetika Capital Partners currently consists of one General Partner and four Limited Partners.

Structural Alignment

The Partnership Structure

Unlike traditional fund managers who separate their own wealth from their clients, Ganetika is structured so that the General Partner and our four Limited Partners invest side-by-side in the exact same fund.

The Ganetika Fund

The Single Investment Pool

All partner contributions are merged into a single partnership pool. There are no separate classes, priority allocations, or tiered structures.

Identical Portfolio Ownership

GP and LPs own exact proportional shares of every underlying business investment.

Zero Principal-Agent Conflict

We succeed only when our partners succeed. We eat our own cooking.

Concentrated Business Focus

Deploying our shared capital into high-quality businesses with long runways for profit creation.

Our Core Principles

Disciplined Approach

We invest with a focus on intrinsic value and a clear margin of safety. Every decision is guided by business fundamentals, sound reasoning, and an insistence on rational capital allocation.

Long-Term Horizon

We think like business owners, not traders. Our goal is to hold outstanding companies that can compound value for decades, allowing time and quality to do the heavy lifting.

Partnership Mindset

We treat our partners’ capital as our own. The General Partner invests alongside the Limited Partners, ensuring alignment of interests and a shared commitment to long-term results.

Local and Global

While based in Indonesia, our search for value knows no borders. We invest where enduring businesses and sensible prices meet—whether at home or abroad.

About the General Partner

Gantan Etika Murty

Founder & General Partner

Gantan holds an Honours degree in Electrical-Mechatronics Engineering from UTM and a Master’s in Management from Imperial College London. He began investing in 2021, guided by a long-term, fundamentals-focused philosophy shaped by the teachings of disciplined investors such as Warren Buffett, Charlie Munger, Philip Fisher, Howard Marks, Peter Lynch, Joel Greenblatt, Seth Klarman, and Li Lu.
